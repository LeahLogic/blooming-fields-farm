Our Farm Story

Founded in 1995, Blooming Fields Farm has been a family-owned operation dedicated to sustainable agriculture and providing the highest quality flowers, berries, and trees to our community.

We practice eco-friendly farming methods that respect the land while producing beautiful and delicious products. Our farm spans over 200 acres of carefully cultivated fields and orchards.

Our Flowers

We grow a wide variety of seasonal flowers perfect for any occasion or simply to brighten your day.

Hydrangea

Hydrangeas are colorful shrubs with large, showy flowers that brighten up any garden.

In Season: June-September
Tulips

Colorful tulips in a rainbow of hues. A spring favorite that signals the start of the season.

In Season: March-May
Lavender

Fragrant lavender perfect for bouquets, sachets, or culinary uses. Grown in our southern fields.

In Season: June-August
Peonies

Lush, fragrant peonies in shades of pink, white, and red. A customer favorite each spring.

In Season: May-June
Sunflowers

Bright, cheerful yellow flowers known for their large size and edible seeds.

In Season: Summer to early Fall
Dahlias

Vibrant, bushy flowers with a variety of shapes and colors. Great for bouquets.

In Season: Summer to early Fall
Snapdragons

Tall spikes of colorful flowers, popular for their unique shape and variety of colors.

In Season: Spring to Fall
Zinnias

Easy-to-grow, bright flowers that attract butterflies and come in many colors.

In Season: June-September

Our Trees

We cultivate a variety of trees perfect for landscaping or simply enjoying on our farm.

Cherry Blossoms

Our orchard features dozens of cherry blossom trees that create a spectacular display each spring.

Harvest: June – July

Apple Trees

Our heritage apple orchard features over 20 varieties, some dating back to the 1800s.

Harvest: August-October

Pear Tree

A deciduous tree known for its sweet, juicy fruits. Pears typically ripen late summer to early fall.

Harvest: August – September
